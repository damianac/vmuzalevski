Structural Repair &
Strengthening Design

Structural Strengthening is the process of upgrading structures to improve performance under existing loads or to increase the strength of structural members to carry additional loads.
Structural Repair changes a property to bring it up to local health and safety standards.

January 2022

Dubai Mall - Loro Piana

Subsequent installation of the elevator caused a new opening in the slab.
To enhance the ultimate/service limit state and to remain the original capacity for the slabs and provide proper supports around the new openings, new RC beams were added.
In order to keep the original strength capacity, carbon fiber were added to compensate influences caused by the new elevator load. RAM Concept software as well as own spreadsheets were used for the design. I increased my self-confidence by providing a good solution.

March 2022

Nad al Sheba Residential Villa
Dubai

Nad al Sheba Residential Villa project has requested to check the existing Roof slab due to the change in function of architecture and to provide strengthening solution where is required.
The structure model was done in SAFE software. It was found that new load caused exceeding the permitted limits such as: punching shear and required reinforcement.
Using techniques like top thickening and Carbon fiber we solved the problem in record time.

March 2022

Creek Views
Dubai

Due to the change in function of architecture, Creek Views project has appointed us to check the existing PT slab at Ground floor and to provide strengthening solution where is required.
New model has generated using RAM Concept software. In addition to the usual solutions, the thickness of the thickening was limited for some columns, therefore the strengthening solution was based on the calculation of studs connected with steel plates from bottom and top sides.
We are very proud of the ideas and solutions we create.

February 2022

Al Qasimiyah School
Sharjah

The project required adequate passive back-propping for the purpose of repair of damaged columns on first and ground floor. The challenge was to transfer the vertical load from the columns so that there were no harmful effects on the surrounding elements.
The back props have designed in MIDAS software.
We have escaped the problems by solving them.

March 2022

ADIA Carpark Project
Abu Dhabi

Carbon fibre plates can be used to improve, increase or repair the performance and resistance of concrete structures. Strategically placed, strengthening plates can be bonded to concrete elements to provide significant increases in carrying capacity and stiffness.
The above-mentioned project required such a solution, where carbon rods were used in addition to carbon plates.
New material create more advanced products and replace materials subject to supply disruptions, like rare earth elements.
